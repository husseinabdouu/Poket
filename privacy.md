# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: February 28, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how **PokerApp** ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information you provide directly
- **Display name** — used to identify you to other players in your groups
- **Email address or phone number** — used for account authentication
- **Profile photo** — optional, displayed to other group members
- **Venmo username** — optional, used to auto-open Venmo for settling poker debts
- **CashApp $cashtag** — optional, used to auto-open CashApp for settling poker debts

### Information generated by your use of the App
- **Poker session data** — buy-in and cash-out amounts you enter for sessions you participate in
- **Group membership** — which poker groups you belong to
- **Notable hands** — any notable poker hands you choose to record

### Information collected automatically
- **Firebase usage data** — Google Firebase collects basic analytics data (e.g. app usage, crash reports) as described in [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Authenticate your identity and maintain your account
- Enable you to create and join poker groups with other players
- Display your statistics and session history to members of your groups
- Facilitate settlements between players by optionally opening Venmo or CashApp
- Provide, maintain, and improve the App

We **do not** sell your personal information to third parties.

---

## 3. How We Share Your Information

### With other group members
Your **display name**, **profile photo**, and **poker session results** are visible to other members of the groups you belong to. This is core to the App's functionality.

### With service providers
We use the following third-party services to operate the App:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---|---|---|
| Google Firebase | Authentication, database, and hosting | [firebase.google.com/support/privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) |
| RevenueCat | Subscription management (if applicable) | [revenuecat.com/privacy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy) |

### Legal requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights, users' safety, or the public.

---

## 4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account through the App, your account and profile data are permanently deleted within 30 days. Session and group data you contributed may remain associated with the group in an anonymised form.

---

## 5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your data (you can do this at any time by going to Profile → Delete Account in the App)
- **Export** your data
- **Object** to certain processing of your data

**EU/UK residents (GDPR):** You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

**California residents (CCPA):** You have the right to know what personal data we collect and request its deletion. We do not sell personal data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **husseinabdou06@gmail.com**.

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
